Physics

See Publications in the sidebar for my papers in theoretical physics. A large part of my past research efforts focussed on supergravity models of cosmology, which manifests as coupling 4d supergravity to a nilpotent multiplet, also known as the so-called de Sitter supergravity. I've also worked a bit on scattering amplitudes, both in extended supergravities and theories where supersymmetry is spontaneously broken.
